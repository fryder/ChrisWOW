Sell Process

Things to know if you want to sell or trade your bike to WOW Motorcycles.

  1. All purchases and trades are subject to inspection for final value.
  2. Title must be in your name to sell the bike
  3. Any lien holder information must be disclosed prior to final purchase
  4. WOW prefers NO salvage or rebuilt title vehicles.
  5. Must show valid photo ID to sell vehicle

All vehicles have to go through a thorough inspection prior to purchase.

The title must be evaluated or if there is a lien holder, the payoff must be verified.

Please allow 30 - 60 minutes for the entire process.
